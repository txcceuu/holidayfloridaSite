Unleash the Adventure: Universal Orlando Resort!

Dive into the epic worlds of Universal Orlando Resort! Experience the magic of Harry Potter, brave the dinosaurs of Jurassic Park, and become part of your favorite movies. Universal offers thrilling rides, immersive environments, and unforgettable entertainment for the whole family.

Universal Studios Florida

Diagon Alley, Minions, Hollywood Rip Ride Rockit.

Islands of Adventure

Hogsmeade, Jurassic Park, The Incredible Hulk Coaster.

Volcano Bay (Water Park)

South Pacific themed water park with a massive volcano.

Splash & Play: Other Fantastic Water Parks

When the Florida sun shines bright, there's no better way to cool down and have a blast than at one of Orlando's amazing water parks. From thrilling slides to lazy rivers, these aquatic wonderlands offer fun for all ages beyond Universal's Volcano Bay.

Typhoon Lagoon (Disney)

Tropical paradise with a huge wave pool.

Blizzard Beach (Disney)

Ski resort themed water park with thrilling slides.

Aquatica (SeaWorld)

Animal encounters mixed with exciting slides.

Explore International Drive: Fun Beyond Parks!

International Drive, or I-Drive, is the vibrant heart of Orlando's entertainment, dining, and shopping scene outside the major theme parks. Discover unique attractions, fantastic restaurants, and endless ways to have fun!

ICON Park

Ride The Wheel, visit Madame Tussauds, SEA LIFE Aquarium.

WonderWorks

An upside-down amusement park for the mind with over 100 exhibits.

Fun Spot America

Family-owned amusement park with roller coasters and go-karts.

Potential Hotels for Your Stay

Orlando offers a vast array of accommodations, from luxurious resorts with theme park perks to spacious family villas and budget-friendly hotels. Here are a few examples to consider for your comfortable stay.

Universal's Cabana Bay Beach Resort

Retro-themed, two pools, bowling alley, walking distance to Volcano Bay.

Hard Rock Hotel Orlando

Rock 'n' roll themed, luxurious, walking distance to Universal Studios.

Marriott's Grande Vista

Spacious villas, golf course, multiple pools, great for families.

Delicious Restaurants to Try

Orlando's dining scene is as diverse as its attractions! From themed eateries to international cuisine and casual family spots, you'll find something to satisfy every craving during your holiday.

The Toothsome Chocolate Emporium

Hard Rock Cafe Orlando

World's largest Hard Rock Cafe, classic American fare and rock memorabilia.

Universal CityWalk Dining

Wide variety of restaurants and entertainment options at Universal's hub.
