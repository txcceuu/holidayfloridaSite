Splash & Play: Orlando's Water Parks

When the Florida sun shines bright, there's no better way to cool down and have a blast than at one of Orlando's amazing water parks. From thrilling slides to lazy rivers, these aquatic wonderlands offer fun for all ages.

Typhoon Lagoon (Disney)

Tropical paradise with a huge wave pool.

Blizzard Beach (Disney)

Ski resort themed water park with thrilling slides.

Volcano Bay (Universal)

South Pacific themed park with a massive volcano.

Aquatica (SeaWorld)

Animal encounters mixed with exciting slides.
